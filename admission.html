Admission Procedure

This section provides detailed information about Delhi Skill and Entrepreneurship University(DSEU) pitampura campus admission procedure; Candidate are advised to check the complete process before applying the form.

Courses Admission Procedure
Diploma in Fashion Design Allocation of seats based on online counselling conducted by dseu. the eligible candidate shall appear for Common Enterence Test conducted bt DSEU. The university will offer admissions through a merit list createdbased on marks obtained by the candidate in CET(Common Enterence Test). in addition, the candidate seeking admission to design-oriented Diploma Courses must have obtained qualifying marks as specified by the University in Section D of CET.
Diploma in Computer/Civil/Electronics Engineering Allocation of seats based on online counselling conducted by dseu. the eligible candidate shall appear for Common Enterence Test conducted bt DSEU. The university will offer admissions through a merit list createdbased on marks obtained by the candidate in CET(Common Enterence Test).

For Letral Entry

The candidate should have passed analogous 12th Vocational (Engg) stream of CBSE or equivalent from Schools located only in Delhi with minimum 60% marks in aggregate of best five subjects for which marks are reflected in the mark sheet.

                                                                                                                               OR

Passed 10th class of CBSE or equivalent + 2 years ITI(Engg) from ITI s/ITCs. The ITI s/ ITCs must be from Institutes located only in Delhi and affiliated to DGE&T/BTE, Delhi with a minimum of 60% marks in aggregate of all subjects reflected in the mark sheet.
