Computer Engineering

Computer Engineering as an advanced and emerging area of technology has shown an unprecedented growth in every sphere of activity. A conscious attempt has been made to design the curriculum as per the requirement and need of the industry. Subjects like OOPS, DBMS, System Design, Multimedia computing etc are being taught besides basic sciences and allied subjects. Six months industrial Training and Project have been made an integral part of the curriculum whereby students can correlate their theoretical knowledge to the practical approach of problem solving. With equal importance given to hardware and software subjects, department maintains well-equipped and modernized DBMS, Software, CAD, Hardware, Multimedia and Networking Laboratories with internet facility. After Successful completion of the course students can secure jobs as Software Engineers, Hardware Engineer, Maintenance Engineer, Sr.Programmers and Computer Faculty.
