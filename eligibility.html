Eligibility

For candidates applying for

Diploma Courses in Computer Engineering, Electronics Engineering (Digital Electronics), Electronics Engineering (Medical Electronics), Fashion Design, Civil Engg. (Public Health & Environmental Engineering)

Minimum Eligibility

The candidate should have passed 10th class of 10+2 System or Matric or any other examination recognized as equivalent to 10th class examination, conducted by CBSE. with minimum 35% aggregate in Science, Mathematics & English. The subjects Science, Mathematics and English are compulsory and the candidate should have passed in each of these three subjects

Note:
1. Candidates passing /qualifying class 10th under the grading system should have obtained minimum pass grade in the compulsory subjects i.e., Science, Mathematics and English.
2. Any other criteria as prescribed by CBSE in new pattern.

Lateral entry in second year diploma course

For admission of 10+2 Vocational Engg. Stream pass outs / ITI pass outs against 10% supernumerary seats in analogous disciplines of Civil Engineering, Civil Engineering (Construction Technology), Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Electronics Engineering (Digital Electronics), Electronics Engineering (Medical Electronics), Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, (Public Health & Environmental Engineering), Information Technology Enabled Services & Management (ITES &M) Tools & Die making.


Minimum Eligibility

The candidate should have passed analogous 12th Vocational (Engg) stream of CBSE or equivalent from Schools located only in Delhi with minimum 60% marks in aggregate of best five subjects for which marks are reflected in the mark sheet.
OR
Passed 10th class of CBSE or equivalent + 2 years ITI(Engg) from ITI s/ITCs. The ITI s/ ITCs must be from Institutes located only in Delhi and affiliated to DGE&T/BTE, Delhi with a minimum of 60% marks in aggregate of all subjects reflected in the mark sheet.
